Genomic Overview

This research project utilizes PacBio HiFi (Long-Read) technology to reconstruct the complex eukaryotic genome of Oryza sativa. Rice is a critical model organism in plant biology, and high-quality de novo assemblies are essential for understanding climate adaptation and yield optimization.

K-mer Profiling (Jellyfish)

Before assembly, we performed k-mer counting to estimate the genome size and check for heterozygosity. This step ensures the data quality is sufficient for Hifiasm.
